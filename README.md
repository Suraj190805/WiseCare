# CareCompanion AI 🧓💙
### Smart Elderly Independence & Safety Platform

CareCompanion AI is an intelligent, AI-driven healthcare companion designed to help elderly individuals live independently, safely, and with dignity. The platform provides real-time health monitoring, medication reminders, emergency alerts, nutrition guidance, and seamless connectivity with caregivers and doctors through a voice-first and easy-to-use interface.

This project is developed for the **AI-Driven Healthcare Hackathon 2025**.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

The elderly population faces major challenges in managing daily health routines and staying safe while living independently.

Key issues include:
- Medication non-adherence
- Delayed emergency response
- Lack of personalized diet guidance
- Social isolation
- Limited access to healthcare monitoring
- Technology and language barriers

CareCompanion AI solves these problems using AI-powered monitoring, reminders, and real-time healthcare support.

---

## 🎯 Project Vision

To build a trusted AI healthcare companion that enables elderly individuals to:

- Live independently
- Stay safe at home
- Maintain proper health routines
- Stay connected with caregivers and doctors
- Receive personalized care through AI
